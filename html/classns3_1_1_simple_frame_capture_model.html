Detailed Description

A simple threshold-based model for frame capture effect. If the new incoming frame arrives while the receiver is receiving the preamble of another frame and the SIR of the new incoming frame is above a fixed margin, then the current frame is dropped and the receiver locks onto the new incoming frame.
