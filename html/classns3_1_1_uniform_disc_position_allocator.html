Detailed Description

Allocate the positions uniformely (with constant density) randomly within a disc.

UniformDiscPositionAllocator allocates the positions randomly within a disc lying on the plane and having center at coordinates and radius . The random positions are chosen such that, for any subset , the expected value of the fraction of points which fall into corresponds to , i.e., to the ratio of the area of the subset to the area of the whole disc.

Note
using UniformDiscPositionAllocator is not equivalent to using a RandomDiscPositionAllocator with a uniformly-distributed radius, since doing that would results in a point distribution which is more dense towards the center of the disc.
