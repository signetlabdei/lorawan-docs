52 /*
53  * Diagram of the end-device-status data structure. One instance of this class
54  * for each ED, that will be identified by its address.
55  *
56  * Public Access:
57  *
58  * (ED address) --- Current device parameters:
59  * - First Receive Window SF and DRRe
60  * - First Receive Window frequency
61  * - Second Window SF and DR
62  * - Second Receive Window frequency
63  * --- Reply
64  * - Need for reply (true/false)
65  * - Updated reply
66  * --- Received Packets
67  * - Received packets list (see below).
68  *
69  *
70  * Private Access:
71  *
72  * (Received packets list) - List of gateways that received the packet (see below)
73  * - SF of the received packet
74  * - Frequency of the received packet
75  * - Bandwidth of the received packet
76  *
77  * (Gateway list) - Time at which the packet was received
78  * - Reception power
79  */
