Detailed Description

The Classic recovery implementation.

Classic recovery refers to the two well-established recovery algorithms, namely, NewReno (RFC 6582) and SACK based recovery (RFC 6675).

The idea of the algorithm is that when we enter recovery, we set the congestion window value to the slow start threshold and maintain it at such value until we are fully recovered (in other words, until the highest sequence transmitted at time of detecting the loss is ACKed by the receiver).
